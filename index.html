About Us

Cheese n Freeze is a multicuisine restaurant that was first started in 2011 at Nanganallur, Chennai by our founder Ashwinath and his friends. CnF now has four branches along with a bakery and confectionary unit called “the most wanted cake shop” and with your love and support, we hope to open more!
